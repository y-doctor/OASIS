Fitness screen re-analysis (v2)

Rerun of the iPSC ligand fitness screen on re-computed MAGeCK outputs. All figures below are at 300 dpi and available as individual PNG downloads (click the image for full size, or use the Download button).

Notes on this run:

• Day-vs-Day4 comparisons use filtered MAGeCK outputs; E6-vs-mTeSR comparisons use unfiltered outputs (filtering removed too many sgRNAs to get stable hit calls at this contrast).

• Gene naming convention in the source data: ligand genes carry _HUMAN suffix (stripped for display); NTCs carry _CONTROL suffix (e.g. AMPR_CONTROL, GLUC_CONTROL, MCHERRY_CONTROL). Note that GLUC_HUMAN (Gaussia luciferase as a ligand) and GLUC_CONTROL (NTC) are both present and treated distinctly.

• Hit definition: |LFC z-score| > 2 and FDR < 0.05. Blue = up, red = down.
